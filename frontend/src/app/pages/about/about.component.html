About CarRental

Our Story

Founded in 2010, CarRental has been at the forefront of the car rental industry in Kenya. What started as a small fleet of 5 vehicles has grown into one of the most trusted names in car rental services across the country.

Our mission is simple: to provide reliable, affordable, and comfortable vehicles that make your journey memorable. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, we ensure you have the perfect vehicle for your needs.
