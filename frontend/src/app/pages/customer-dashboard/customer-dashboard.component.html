Booking and Usage Rules

1. Booking Rules

  • You can only book cars that are available for your selected dates.
  • A booking is reserved only after payment is made.
  • Bookings must be made at least 2 hours before the pickup time.
  • Minimum rental period is 1 hour; maximum is 30 days.

2. Payment & Refund Rules

  • All bookings must be paid before confirmation.
  • Refunds are only processed for cancellations made before the pickup time (according to our policy).

3. Car Usage Rules

  • Return the car on or before the agreed return time to avoid late fees.
  • Late returns will attract additional charges per hour.
  • Car must be returned with the same fuel level as at pickup.
  • Report any damage or unusual car condition immediately.

4. Profile & Verification Rules

  • You must verify your phone number and email before making a booking.
  • A valid ID and driving license are required before renting a car.
  • Keep your contact information updated to receive booking alerts.

5. Review & Feedback Rules

  • You can only review cars that you have rented.
  • Reviews should reflect your genuine experience.
  • Offensive or false reviews will be removed.

6. Cancellation Rules

  • You may cancel a booking before the pickup time.
  • A cancellation fee may apply depending on our policy.
  • Refunds (if eligible) will be processed within 48 hours.

7. General System Rules

  • Misuse of the system or fraudulent bookings will lead to account suspension.
  • Your account will be logged out after 30 minutes of inactivity for security.
  • All activities on your account are monitored for safety.
