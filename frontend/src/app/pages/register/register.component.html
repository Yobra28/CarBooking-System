Booking and Usage Rules

1. Booking Rules

  • You can only book cars that are available for your selected dates.
  • Bookings must be made at least 2 hours before the pickup time.
  • Minimum rental period is 1 hour; maximum is 30 days.

2. Payment & Refund Rules

  • All bookings must be paid before confirmation.
  • Refunds are only processed for cancellations made before the pickup time (according to our policy).

3. Car Usage Rules

  • Return the car on or before the agreed return time to avoid late fees.
  • Late returns will attract additional charges per hour.
  • Return car with the same fuel level as at pickup.

4. Verification Rules

  • A valid ID and driving license are required before renting a car.

5. Cancellation Rules

  • You may cancel a booking before the pickup time. Refund policy may apply.
